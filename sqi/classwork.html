Why Choose SQI
College of ICT?


At SQI College of ICT, we are dedicated to
nurturing the next generation of tech leaders. Our
National Diploma (ND) programs are designed to
provide students with the practical skills and
knowledge needed to excel in the rapidly evolving
tech industry.


With a focus on 80% hands-on training, our
students are not only prepared for the job market
but also equipped to secure remote jobs even
before graduation.


Get the skills

SQI integrates quality, convenience, and modern technology into
education, bridging the gap between theory and real-world
application.


Get the certicate

The National Diploma (ND) offered at SQI College of ICT is a 2 year
approved academic program of the National Board for Technical
Education (NBTE).


Get the job

78.5% of our students found secure employment within three
months of graduation. Students leave from learning to getting job
roles.
